Research

My goal is to push the boundaries of human visual experience through advancements in imaging and display technologies. I am interested in how light captures and conveys visual information, with a focus on computational imaging , optics, and machine learning.

LCoS-based Light Field Projector
Wei-Shan Weng, Ao Yi Sim, Chi-Feng Lee, Homer H. Chen,
SPIE AR | VR | MR , 2025   (Oral Presentation)

The proposed LCoS-based light field projector, equipped with an innovative polarization-alternating beam splitter, projects a crosstalk-free light field with a brightness of 500,000 nits.

3D Phase Imaging using Moire Metalens and Deep Learning

The proposed system integrates an Moire imaging system that has tunable focus depth and a deep Learning model for phase reconstruction.

Physics Informed Neural Network for Ultrasound Imaging

The proposed neural network predicts 2D pressure fields over time by incorporating partial differential equations for acoustic wave propagation into the network’s loss function.

Auto-focusing for Dual-Modality Imaging System

The developed algorithm uses image processing techniques to automatically locate the sample’s position in both axial and lateral directions.
